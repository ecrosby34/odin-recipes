Pasta Bolognese Recipe

Home Page

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese, or simply Bolognese sauce, is a classic Italian pasta dish that originated in the city of Bologna. It is a rich and hearty sauce made with ground meat, tomatoes, aromatic vegetables, and a blend of herbs, served over cooked spaghetti. Here's a description of the components:

  1. Bolognese Sauce: The heart of the dish is the Bolognese sauce, which is a slow-cooked meat sauce. The sauce typically includes a combination of ground beef and pork, although variations may use veal or other meats. The meat is browned to develop flavor before being simmered in a savory mixture of tomatoes, tomato paste, broth, or wine.

  2. Aromatics and Vegetables: Aromatic ingredients like onions, garlic, and celery are finely chopped and sautéed to create the flavor base for the Bolognese sauce. Carrots may also be included to add sweetness and depth to the sauce.

  3. Tomatoes: The sauce is enriched with tomatoes, either in the form of canned crushed tomatoes or tomato puree. This contributes acidity and a vibrant red color to the sauce.

  4. Herbs and Seasonings: Bolognese sauce is seasoned with a combination of herbs such as oregano, basil, and thyme. Bay leaves may be added for additional depth. Salt and pepper are used to enhance the overall flavor.

  5. Milk or Cream: A unique aspect of authentic Bolognese sauce is the addition of milk or cream. This adds a creamy richness to the sauce while balancing the acidity of the tomatoes.

  6. Spaghetti: The sauce is traditionally served over cooked spaghetti, though other pasta shapes can be used. The pasta is cooked al dente, providing a firm texture that complements the robust Bolognese sauce.

  7. Parmesan Cheese: Grated Parmesan cheese is often sprinkled on top of the finished dish for added flavor. It brings a salty and nutty element to the overall taste.

To prepare Spaghetti Bolognese, the sauce is simmered for an extended period to allow the flavors to meld and intensify. The result is a deeply flavorful, savory sauce that coats the pasta beautifully. Spaghetti Bolognese is a beloved comfort food that has gained popularity worldwide for its hearty and satisfying qualities.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1/2 pound (225g) ground pork (or use all beef)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, finely chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional)
  • 1 can (28 ounces/800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (optional, for added richness)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish
  • 1 pound (450g) pasta (spaghetti or your choice)

Instructions

  1. Sauté Aromatics:

    • In a large, heavy-bottomed pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions, carrots, celery, and garlic. Sauté until the vegetables are softened.

  2. Brown the Meat:

    • Add ground beef and pork to the pot. Cook until the meat is browned, breaking it apart with a spoon as it cooks.

  3. Deglaze with Wine (optional):

    • Pour in red wine (if using), and let it simmer for a few minutes, scraping any browned bits from the bottom of the pot.

  4. Add Tomatoes and Paste:

    • Stir in crushed tomatoes and tomato paste. Mix well to combine.

  5. Simmer with Milk and Herbs:

    • Pour in the whole milk and add dried oregano, basil, thyme, bay leaves, salt, and pepper. Stir and let the sauce come to a simmer.

  6. Simmer Slowly:

    • Reduce the heat to low and let the Bolognese sauce simmer for at least 1-2 hours, stirring occasionally. The longer it simmers, the richer the flavors will be.

  7. Cook Pasta:

    • Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

  8. Finish the Sauce:

    • If using, stir in heavy cream for added richness. Adjust seasoning to taste.

  9. Serve:

    • Remove bay leaves and discard. Serve the Bolognese sauce over cooked pasta. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh parsley.

Enjoy your homemade Pasta Bolognese! This flavorful and hearty dish is perfect for a comforting meal at home!
